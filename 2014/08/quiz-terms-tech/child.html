4. When everyone at your tech startup is required to use the product/tool/app you are creating, it’s called ______.

  • A "Mary Kay" command
  • Dogfooding Intended to mean that you have to use your own product to validate its quality. If your company makes dog food, you should feed it to your own pet. For example, in 1980, Apple sent out this memo: “EFFECTIVE IMMEDIATELY!! NO MORE TYPEWRITERS ARE TO BE PURCHASED, LEASED, etc., etc. Apple is an innovative company. We must believe and lead in all areas. If word processing is so neat, then let's all use it!”
