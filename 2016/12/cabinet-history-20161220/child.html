Cabinet Secretaries’ Prior Experience

This chart shows the main fields that different presidents’ initial Cabinet members have come from. In most cases we have chosen just one area that the person was known for or had spent the most time in. In a few, we had to double up (Jack Kemp was known to many Americans as a former football player, not just an elected official).
