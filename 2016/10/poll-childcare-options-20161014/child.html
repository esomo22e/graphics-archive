Most Parents Don’t Have Many Child Care Options

Share of parents who say they had a lot, several, just a few realistic care options or only one realistic care option for their child.

Source: NPR/Robert Wood Johnson Foundation/Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health poll of 1,120 parents or guardians of a child 5 years old or younger not yet in kindergarten who receives regularly scheduled care at least once a week from someone other than a parent. The poll was conducted June 8 through Aug. 7, 2016. Margin of error: +/- 3.4 percentage points.
