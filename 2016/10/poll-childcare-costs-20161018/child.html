Child Care Costs Represent A Financial Burden For Some Parents

Nearly a third (31 percent) of parents who have a fee for child care say that the costs have caused a financial problem for their household. Of those, 71 percent say that these costs have represented a “very serious” or “somewhat serious” financial problem.

Notes

“Don’t know” and “Refused” responses are not included.

Source: NPR/Robert Wood Johnson Foundation/Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health poll of 1,120 parents or guardians of a child 5 years old or younger not yet in kindergarten who receives regularly scheduled care at least once a week from someone other than a parent. Margin of error: +/- 3.4 percentage points.This question was asked of the 242 respondents who said that child care costs had caused a financial problem for their household. Margin of error: +/- 7.2 percentage points.The poll was conducted June 8 through Aug. 7, 2016.
