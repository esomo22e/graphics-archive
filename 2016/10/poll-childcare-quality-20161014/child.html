Majority Of Parents Rate Their Child Care As Excellent

Share of parents rating their child care as “excellent” on individual features of quality.

Notes

1. Asked of a subset of the survey group: 684 parents of a child three to five years old
2. Asked of a subset of the survey group: 851 parents of a child cared for by someone other than a relative

Source: NPR/Robert Wood Johnson Foundation/Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health poll of 1,120 parents or guardians of a child 5 years old or younger not yet in kindergarten who receive regularly scheduled care at least once a week from someone other than a parent. The poll was conducted June 8 through Aug. 7, 2016. Margin of error: +/- 3.4 percentage points.
