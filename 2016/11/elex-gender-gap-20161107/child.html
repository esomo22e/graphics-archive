The 2016 Gender Gap Could Be The Largest In At Least Six Decades

The latest exit polls show Clinton leading by 12 percentage points among women and Trump leading by 12 percentage points among men. A 24-point gap could be the largest in more than 60 years.

Notes

— Data for 2016 are the latest available, as of 6 AM on Nov. 9. — Data through 1972 are based on Gallup’s final pre-election polls.

Source: Gallup (1952 through 1972), Roper Center (1976 through 2012), 2016 exit polls via CNN
