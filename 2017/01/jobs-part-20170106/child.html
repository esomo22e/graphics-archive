Many Part-Time Workers Who Wanted More Hours Finally Got Them

Below is the share of workers who are “part-time for economic reasons” — that is, who are part time but would rather be working full-time. They currently make up around 3.7 percent of all workers. That’s down 2 full percentage points from when Obama took office. However, it still hasn’t fully returned to pre-recession levels.

Source: Federal Reserve Bank of St. Louis
